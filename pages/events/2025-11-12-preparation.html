Preparation for Christmas at Casa Sale

Wednesday, November 12, 2025

Event Details

Sale Preparation

Date

November 12, 2025

Time

7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Location

CASA, 230 8th Street South
Lethbridge, AB, T1J 5H2

Type

Sale Preparation

About This Event

Join us for a preparation session for our upcoming Christmas at Casa Sale. This is a collaborative effort to organize, price, and prepare all the beautiful textile art pieces that will be featured in our holiday sale.

This session is essential for ensuring our sale runs smoothly and showcases the incredible work of our guild members in the best possible way.

What We'll Be Doing

  • Artwork organization: Sorting and categorizing pieces for the sale
  • Pricing coordination: Establishing fair and consistent pricing
  • Display planning: Planning the layout and presentation of items
  • Inventory management: Creating lists and tracking systems
  • Sale logistics: Coordinating volunteer schedules and responsibilities

What to Bring

  • Your artwork pieces for the sale (properly labeled with your name)
  • Any display materials or props you'd like to use
  • Pricing information for your pieces
  • Notebook for taking notes and coordinating
  • Your enthusiasm for making this sale a success!

Important Notes

This preparation session is crucial for the success of our Christmas sale. Please come prepared with your pieces and any specific requirements or preferences for display. We'll work together to create an amazing showcase of our guild's talent.
