About This Event

Join us for our annual Christmas at Casa Sale, featuring an incredible collection of handmade textile art from our talented guild members. This is a perfect opportunity to find unique, handcrafted gifts for the holiday season.

Our sale showcases a diverse range of textile art including embellished pieces, fabric dying works, mixed media creations, modern felt art, and printed textiles. Each piece is carefully crafted by our guild members and represents the highest quality of textile artistry.

What You'll Find

  • Embellished textiles: Beautiful pieces with intricate details and embellishments
  • Fabric art: Dyed and painted fabrics showcasing various techniques
  • Mixed media works: Creative combinations of materials and techniques
  • Modern felt art: Contemporary felt pieces with innovative designs
  • Printed textiles: Unique printed fabrics and surface designs
  • Featured works: Special pieces highlighting our members' finest creations

Event Highlights

  • Meet the artists and learn about their creative processes
  • Enjoy refreshments while browsing the collection
  • Find unique, one-of-a-kind gifts for the holiday season
  • Support local artists and the textile arts community
  • Discover new techniques and inspiration for your own creative journey

Payment Information

We accept cash and major credit cards. For questions about purchasing artwork, please email surfacedesignlethbridge@gmail.com to coordinate payment and pickup arrangements.
